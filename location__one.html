Continuation
Diameter of this planet is around 15,000 km which is slightly larger than earth. Atmosphere contains oxygen, hydrogen sulfide, nitrogen and carbon dioxide. The ocean underneath seems to be continously moving allowing distribution of resources. On our previous dive we identified the existence of cold seeps which releases methane that is transformed into sugar by microbes. Other teams have begun to arrive and have started building human settlements.
The Surface
Not a lot of oxygen the lower we get into the depths of the ocean. Only mapped out around 100 square feet of the place and then reported back to the base. More resources are being allocated towards the building of the base.
Beyond the Ice
Habitats are being built from transporting materials from Earth or nearby bases to the planet. It's a costy process but necessary. The light from the sun is used for generating energy. Large scale solar panels are being constructed to soak up as much light as possible. They are now being dubbed as: "light farms." Cities must be as insulated as possible so they are padded as much as possible and plants are grown inside. As of right now there is only Base 0, but we are planning to expand once things stabilize. Hopefully, after building homes we can introduce more people.
