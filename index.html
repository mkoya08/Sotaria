Surviving without Sunlight

What is Chemosynthesis?

Chemosythesis is described as the process in which energy is generated without the use of sunlight. This is done in the deep ocean where sunlight rarely penetrates. Therefore, it became necessary for other processes to form. Hydrothermal vents spew out resources like hydrogen sulfide and oxygen that are then used by microbes like bacteria and archaea to create sugar. That sugar is then taken up by tubeworms that use it as food. This allows for a vibrant community to be created even without the life energy of sunlight.
